The Coordinating Circle

Volunteer-led governance and operational leadership for Inner Journey Canada

Behind the Scenes

Inner Journey Canada operates through seven volunteer committees. The committee coordinators, elected at the Annual General Meeting, comprise the Coordinating Circle, which provides business and operational leadership, and through liaison with the Facilitator Group.

The Circle welcomes feedback and agenda items. Monthly meetings are open to interested members. IJ graduates can volunteer with committees or attend Circle meetings by contacting the Chair.
