The Inner Journey Intensive

A Journey Into Greater Power, Passion, Presence and Peace

Many of us spend our time focused on our outer journey through life, seeking satisfaction and success through external sources: money, material things, jobs, relationships. While there is nothing wrong with having these things, and while we may experience feelings of achievement, they are often fleeting. A deeper part of ourselves remains unfulfilled.

The Inner Journey Intensive is an opportunity to explore those deeper parts of yourself and to:

  • Create awareness
  • Deepen awareness of your inner self
  • Open your heart to compassion and love
  • Enrich your relationships with self and others
  • Appreciate the gifts of the moment
  • Create new possibilities for the future

We welcome people of all races, ethnicities and sexual orientation, and our facility can accommodate people with disabilities. We honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions. The curriculum is designed to enable people to develop their own path for inner work.

About the Retreat

The Inner Journey Intensive is offered for participants aged 18 and older by Inner Journey Canada, a non-profit, educational organization supported by transformational professionals and committed volunteers. Every volunteer has participated in this program at least once.

The Intensive is a 3.5-day residential retreat, beginning on a Thursday evening and ending Sunday evening. It is held at a spacious and fully accessible facility on the beautiful shores of the Big Rideau Lake near Perth, Ontario — about a 1 h 25 drive from Ottawa.

A team of highly trained facilitators creates the space and structure for your journey, supported by a full team of volunteers — a 1:4 facilitator-to-participant ratio that ensures personal attention throughout. Our chef provides healthy and delicious meals, and can accommodate most dietary requirements and needs.
