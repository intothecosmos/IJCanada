Programs & Retreats

Find the transformational experience that's right for you.

Not Sure Where to Start?

All of our programs are powerful catalysts for transformation. The right program for you depends on where you are on your journey, what questions you're asking, and what kind of experience resonates with you. Here's what to consider:

  • Are you beginning a journey of inner work or deepening an existing practice?
  • Do you want intensive immersion or a shorter workshop format?
  • Are you seeking personal transformation, healing, or facilitator training?
  • What time commitment works for your schedule?

Browse our programs below, or reach out to discuss which program might be the best fit for you.

5 Elements Movement Meditation

A soulful practice of movement, breath, and meditation bringing awareness to the five energies of the Taoist tradition — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water. Develop presence, vitality, and a deeper connection to your body and the natural world.

Frequency: Periodically throughout the year

Workshop 2 Days Beginner Friendly

Family Constellation Journey

Based on the work of German psychotherapist Bert Hellinger, this workshop examines life challenges through the lens of family and ancestral patterns. Combined with Inner Journey methods, it offers a powerful opportunity to find lasting solutions and end deep suffering.

Frequency: Once yearly (winter)
