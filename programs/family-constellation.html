Family Constellation Journey

We all share one common experience – we are born into a family. Developed by German psychotherapist Bert Hellinger, this methodology examines life challenges through family and ancestral lenses. The program combines Family Constellation principles with Inner Journey methods — an immense opportunity to find lasting solutions and end our deepest suffering.

Who Is This For?

Family Constellation Journey is designed for people ready to understand and heal their family patterns. You might be drawn to this program if:

  • You notice recurring patterns in your relationships that seem to repeat family dynamics
  • You're curious about the hidden loyalties or inherited beliefs shaping your choices
  • You have difficult family relationships and want to find more peace and understanding
  • You've done personal work and want to go deeper into family system roots
  • You're interested in how family history influences who you are today
  • You want to honor your family while freeing yourself from limiting patterns
  • You're ready to transform your relationship to your parents, siblings, or family legacy

Best suited for people who have done some personal work or therapy. Some familiarity with emotional processing is helpful, though not required.

What Is Family Constellation Work?

The Core Principle

Family Constellation work is based on the insight that every person belongs to a family system—and that system continues to influence us, often in ways we don't consciously recognize. Our parents, siblings, grandparents, and even people who died before we were born can influence our beliefs, choices, and life patterns. Family Constellation helps us see these connections and find peace with them.

How It Works

In a Family Constellation workshop, participants work with a facilitator and use representatives (other workshop participants) to enact their family system. By observing these representations, hidden patterns become visible. Specific phrases and movements help participants complete what was left unfinished, honor what needs honoring, and move toward freedom and peace.

What Makes It Powerful

Family Constellation work is powerful because:

  • It makes the invisible visible. Unconscious patterns become clear when enacted in the room.
  • It works at a systemic level. Rather than focusing only on individual psychology, it addresses the family field itself.
  • It creates immediate shifts. Many people experience profound relief and clarity during or shortly after a constellation.
  • You benefit even as a witness. You don't need to have a constellation for yourself—witnessing others' work can activate your own healing.
  • It honors what is. Rather than judging or trying to change family members, it helps you see and accept them as they truly are.

What Happens in a Workshop

Before the Workshop

Think about what issue, relationship, or pattern you'd like to explore. You don't need to have it all figured out—even a vague sense of "something doesn't feel right in my family" is enough to begin.

Opening Circle

We begin by gathering as a group, setting intention, and creating a safe container for the work. The facilitator explains how constellations work and answers any questions.

Your Constellation

If you choose to have a constellation, you'll meet briefly with the facilitator to share your issue or question. You'll then select representatives from the group to stand in for members of your family system. As you position them in the room, patterns become visible. The facilitator uses specific phrases and movements to help shift the system toward resolution and peace.

Witnessing Others' Work

Even if you don't have your own constellation, witnessing others is deeply powerful. Watching someone else's family system often illuminates your own. Many people report that the constellation they needed to see was someone else's.

Closing & Integration

We close with time for reflection and integration. Many people prefer to sit in silence, journaling or simply being with what emerged.
