Inner Journey Canada

A non-profit, educational organization committed to creating environments in which people can learn and grow, emotionally and spiritually, and apply this development to daily living.

Explore Our Programs View Calendar

Inner Journey Canada, the Canadian-based affiliate of the U.S.-based Inner Journey Institute, welcomes people of all backgrounds. We are not affiliated with any religious or spiritual institution or organization, and we honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions.

We welcome people of all races, ethnicities and sexual orientation. Our facilities are accessible to and usable by people with disabilities.

5 Elements Movement Meditation

A practice that combines movement, breath, and meditation grounded in traditional wisdom. Develop presence, ease, and a deeper connection to your body and the natural world. Perfect for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Family Constellation Journey

Explore and heal family patterns that may be unconsciously influencing your life. Through dynamic group work, gain insight into inherited patterns and find new ways of relating to your family system and yourself.
