Thank You — Your Registration Is In

We've received your registration for the Inner Journey Intensive. Take a deep breath — the first step is done.

What happens next

  1. You'll receive a confirmation email within the next few days acknowledging your registration.
  2. That email will include payment information and details about securing your spot with a $250 non-refundable deposit.
  3. Closer to the retreat, we'll send you preparation materials, a packing list, and travel details for Easter Seals Camp Merrywood in Perth, Ontario.
  4. If you indicated you'd like a call, one of our coordinators will reach out to answer questions and help you prepare.

Questions in the meantime? Email inner.journey.canada@gmail.com or call 647-850-9337.
