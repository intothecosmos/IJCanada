About Michael

A former Buddhist monk, policeman, and therapist, Michael has been facilitating inner work since 1982. He founded Inner Journey seminars and facilitates transformational seminars internationally.

Experience & Credentials

  • Former Buddhist monk
  • Former policeman
  • Licensed therapist
  • Facilitating inner work since 1982

Approach

Michael's facilitation blends psychology, spiritual wisdom, and lived experience. He creates safe, supportive environments where deep personal work can unfold naturally. His approach honors both intellectual understanding and embodied transformation, helping people not just think differently, but live differently.

Programs

Michael facilitates:

  • Inner Journey Intensive — 3.5-day residential retreat for deep personal discovery
  • Facilitator Program — Training for those called to support others' transformation
