Workshops & Personal Development Programs in Ottawa-Gatineau

Accessible community-based programs for growth and transformation

Inner Journey Canada brings transformational personal development programs directly to the Ottawa-Gatineau community. Whether you're seeking to deepen your meditation practice, explore family patterns, or explore your potential for growth, we offer accessible workshops and programs that meet you where you are.

Our Community Programs

5 Elements Movement Meditation

Embody transformation through integrated movement, breath, and meditation. This ongoing workshop series combines the wisdom of body-based practices with mindfulness to create lasting change.

Regular sessions in Ottawa-Gatineau area

Learn More

Family Constellation Journey

Explore and heal deeper family patterns, inherited dynamics, and unresolved relationships. This powerful group work offers profound insights and emotional healing through constellation workshops.

Periodic workshops throughout the year

Learn More

The Residential Retreat Option

For those seeking deeper immersion and transformational work, the Inner Journey Intensive is a 3.5-day residential retreat held just 90 minutes from Ottawa. This experiential program combines individual discovery, group work, movement, and reflection to create lasting personal change.

Who Our Programs Are For

Our workshops and programs are for adults seeking personal growth, emotional healing, spiritual development, and deeper connection—whether you're just beginning your journey or continuing work you've started elsewhere. We welcome people of all backgrounds, traditions, and experience levels.

New to personal growth work? Our workshops are accessible entry points designed to support beginners.

Already on a journey? Our programs complement existing practices and deepen your work.

Seeking healing or resolution? Our experienced facilitators create safe containers for meaningful work.

Looking for community? Our programs build genuine connection with others on similar paths.
