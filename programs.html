Programs & Retreats

Find the transformational experience that's right for you. Whether you're beginning your journey or deepening a long-standing practice, we offer programs designed for every stage and readiness level.

Not Sure Where to Start?

All of our programs are powerful catalysts for transformation. The right program for you depends on where you are on your journey, what questions you're asking, and what kind of experience resonates with you. Here's what to consider:

  • Are you beginning a journey of inner work or deepening an existing practice?
  • Do you want intensive immersion or a shorter workshop format?
  • Are you seeking personal transformation, healing, or facilitator training?
  • What time commitment works for your schedule?

Browse our programs below, or reach out to discuss which program might be the best fit for you.

FLAGSHIP PROGRAM

Inner Journey Intensive

Our most transformational offering—a 3-day residential retreat designed to help you step out of everyday patterns and into deep inner work. An immersive experience for real, lasting change.

Format: 3-day residential retreat
Location: Ottawa/Gatineau area
Experience Required: None—beginners welcome

Facilitated group process, personal inquiry, experiential exercises, community connection. A turning point for many participants.

Learn More

5 Elements Movement Meditation

Discover your body's wisdom through a gentle yet profound practice combining movement, breath, and meditation rooted in traditional wisdom.

Format: 1-2 day workshop
Location: Ottawa/Gatineau area
Experience Required: None—all levels welcome

Develop presence, ease, and deeper connection to your body. Grounded in timeless practices adapted for modern life.

Learn More

Family Constellation Journey

Explore and heal family patterns that may be influencing your life. Gain insight into inherited dynamics and discover new ways of relating to your family and yourself.

Format: 1-2 day workshop
Location: Ottawa/Gatineau area
Experience Required: Basic familiarity with personal work

Dynamic group work, individual representations, deep family system insights. Healing at the roots.

Learn More

Facilitator Training Program

For experienced practitioners ready to support others. Our comprehensive facilitator training develops both your personal practice and your capacity to guide others through transformation.

Format: Multi-phase intensive program
Location: Ottawa/Gatineau area
Experience Required: Minimum 1 year experience with personal work

Personal mastery, facilitation skills, program knowledge, ethical practice. Launch a new path.
