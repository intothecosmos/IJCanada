Retreat Details

Dates

May 7-10, 2026 (Spring)
October 22-25, 2026 (Fall)
Thursday evening (~6 p.m.) through Sunday evening (~6 p.m.)

Location

Easter Seals Camp Merrywood
Big Rideau Lake, Perth, Ontario
Approximately 1 hour 25 minutes from Ottawa

Requirements

Ages 18+. Open to all backgrounds, races, ethnicities, and sexual orientations. We honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths.

Format

3.5-day residential retreat combining body, mind, and spirit work. An immersive experience of personal discovery featuring depth, adventure, movement, and celebration.

Includes

Meals, dorm accommodation, all program materials, and a follow-up reunion with your cohort.

Facilitators & Staff

2-to-1 facilitator-to-participant ratio ensuring personal attention and deep support throughout.
Led by JF Sauriol, Maria Marrongelli, Jack Lafleur, and Synandra Lechner.
