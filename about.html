About Inner Journey Canada

A non-profit, educational organization creating environments for learning, growth, and transformation

Our Story

Inner Journey Canada is the Canadian-based affiliate of the U.S.-based Inner Journey Institute, a non-profit, educational organization committed to creating environments in which people can learn and grow, emotionally and spiritually, and apply this development to daily living.

What is the Inner Journey?

In the early nineties, Michael Schiesser created with his friend Franz Friedrich a program called 'The Quest' which became the forerunner of the Inner Journey. The first Inner Journey Seminar was developed by Michael in the summer of 1997, based on his personal studies in the fields of psychology, anthropology, philosophy and with spiritual teachers of the major spiritual streams of East and West. Major influences on the design of the Inner Journey came from Jungian based psychology, the Five Element Theory of ancient Chinese philosophy, the Human Potential Movement and Transpersonal Psychology.

In 1999/2000 Michael met his present partners in teaching the Inner Journey, Neelama Eyres, Howard Ross, Gunnar Nilsson, Penelope Bell, Gerry Guiliani and Jack LaFleur. All of the above mentioned and many more, generously and profoundly contributed to the program and the establishment of 4 communities, San Francisco, Washington DC, Chicago and Ottawa, Canada.

We are not affiliated with any religious or spiritual institution, and we honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions.

The Inner Journey Institute

Inner Journey Canada is part of the broader Inner Journey Institute movement, with roots across North America. Our programs draw from decades of experience, wisdom, and proven methodologies developed within the Institute community. This connection allows us to offer experienced facilitation while remaining deeply rooted in the Ottawa/Gatineau region.

Our Facilitators

Typically, one man and one woman from the list below co-facilitate the Inner Journey Intensive, supported by several other facilitators or trainees and a full complement of staff.

Michael Schiesser

M.A. (Psychology)

A former Buddhist monk, policeman, and therapist, Michael has been facilitating inner work groups since 1982. He created the Inner Journey seminars out of his desire to assist people on their paths towards awakening. As the founder of Inner Journey Seminars, Michael is responsible for course design, facilitator training, program development and holding the Inner Journey vision. He facilitates transformational seminars in the U.S., Canada and Europe.

Neelama Eyers

B.A. (Comparative Religion)

Neelama Eyres' vision, passion, and joy revolve around assisting people on their journeys of "remembering who they are." Her background includes a B.A. in Comparative Religion, the study of Eastern spirituality and Western psychology, leading meditation retreats, facilitating seminars and trainings, private coaching for individuals, couples, and teams, and co-authoring a book. Neelama's training began in 1995, in direct hands-on sessions with students in personal growth intensives. Since 1998, she has worked in the field of personal transformation in the U.S., Canada, and Europe, as a life coach, facilitator, and trainer in transformational workshops—now under the umbrella of Inner Journey Seminars, an educational organization of which she is co-founder.

Jean-François "JF" Sauriol

B.Eng., M.Eng. (Management)

After many years as an officer in the Canadian military and high-tech entrepreneur, JF is now an Inner Journey Coach and Facilitator. JF began a journey of spiritual and personal growth in 1997 and sharing this desire with his wife of 27 years, personal awareness and compassion became the foundation of his parenting 3 kids who are now blossoming young adults. Applying this vision in his personal, business world and in his martial arts practice continues to bring meaning and passion in all facets of life. JF helps clients grow in awareness of their very own body-mind-emotion-spirit combination and foster compassion for their human-ness so that lasting transformation can take place in their "self", in their day-to-day lives and in their relationships at home and at work.

Maria Marrongelli

Maria Marrongelli has a background in psychology and alternative health and has been running a successful private practice in transformational coaching and bodywork since 2001. She has also been facilitating experiential workshops both in the US and Canada since 2006. Maria has helped many people over the past 12 years to understand and experience the connection between body, emotions and mind as a way of moving beyond the limitations of human conditioning. Her work is fueled by her heart's desire for everyone to fully live their own unique expression and awaken to their authentic self.

Jack Lafleur

M.A. (Counselling)

A former military and high school chaplain and long time spiritual seeker, Jack has been facilitating personal and spiritual growth since 1993. He brings to the Inner Journey a wide range of experience which includes organizational health, spiritual direction, the study of martial arts, working with trauma survivors, men's issues, teaching various forms of meditation and Ken Wilber's Integral Model. Jack's work with people comes from a deep trust in transformational work and in a firm committment towards helping others awaken to their essential spiritual nature and fullness of psychological health. Jack currently works as an EAP counsellor and individual, couple and family therapist in private practice.
