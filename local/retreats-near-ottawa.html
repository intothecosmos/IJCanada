Personal Growth Retreats Near Ottawa & Gatineau

Transformational residential retreats just 90 minutes from home

Why Choose a Local Retreat?

Life in the Ottawa-Gatineau region moves fast. We work, we commute, we manage families and responsibilities. Yet many of us feel disconnected from our deeper selves—wondering if there's more meaning, purpose, or peace to discover.

A transformational retreat doesn't require traveling far or taking extensive time away. Inner Journey Canada offers personal growth retreats right here near Ottawa and Gatineau, where you can step away from daily distractions, immerse fully in meaningful work, and return home renewed—all without the burden of distant travel.

Staying close to the region means easier logistics: skip the long flights, arrive refreshed, and invest that time and energy into your own transformation. Our retreats are held at Easter Seals Camp Merrywood on Big Rideau Lake, approximately 90 minutes from Ottawa and Gatineau—far enough to truly disconnect, close enough to manage.

About Inner Journey Canada

Inner Journey Canada is a non-profit, educational organization dedicated to creating environments for emotional and spiritual growth. With nearly three decades of experience in the broader Inner Journey Institute movement and facilitators whose personal work in transformation spans over 40 years, we bring depth, expertise, and genuine care to every retreat we offer.

We are not affiliated with any religious or spiritual institution. We honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions. Whether you're seeking healing, self-discovery, renewed purpose, or deeper connection, our retreats meet you where you are—without dogma, without judgment, only genuine support for your journey.

What Makes Our Retreats Different

2-to-1 Facilitator Ratio

We maintain exceptional facilitator-to-participant ratios, ensuring every person receives genuine attention and personal support throughout the retreat.

Residential Format

You stay on-site in supportive community, immersed in the experience. This allows deeper work and meaningful connection with others on similar journeys.

Integrated Approach

We work with your whole self—body, mind, and spirit—through movement, reflection, group work, and individual inquiry. No single modality, but a holistic journey.

Experienced Facilitators

Our facilitation team brings decades of combined experience in personal transformation, psychology, counselling, and spiritual direction.

Getting There from Ottawa & Gatineau

From Ottawa

Approximately 1 hour 25 minutes

Take the Queensway (Highway 417) east, then connect to Hwy 7 toward Perth. Clear, manageable drive with parking on-site.

From Gatineau

Approximately 1 hour 35 minutes

Cross into Ontario via the bridges, then follow directions toward Perth and Big Rideau Lake. Easy navigation, no complex urban driving.
