Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Inner Journey Canada is a non-profit, educational organization committed to creating environments in which people can learn and grow, emotionally and spiritually, and apply this development to daily living. We are the Canadian-based affiliate of the U.S.-based Inner Journey Institute. We offer transformational retreats, workshops, and circles designed to help individuals discover their authentic selves, heal patterns, and deepen their connection to community.

Our facilitators bring diverse backgrounds and expertise. Our founder and lead facilitator, Michael Schiesser, holds an M.A. in Psychology and has been facilitating transformational experiences since 1982. His experience includes time as a Buddhist monk, police officer, and licensed therapist. Our team also includes professionals with credentials in comparative religion, counselling, psychology, alternative health, and business. Learn more about our facilitators.

Our programs are primarily based in the Ottawa/Gatineau area. The Inner Journey Intensive is held at Easter Seals Camp Merrywood on Big Rideau Lake near Perth, Ontario, approximately 1 hour 25 minutes from Ottawa. Other workshops and circles are held at community spaces in the region. Specific venue information is provided upon registration.

No prior experience is necessary. Our programs welcome complete beginners as well as experienced practitioners. We design our workshops to be accessible to people at all stages of their journey. If you have specific concerns or questions about whether a particular program is right for you, please contact us.

No, Inner Journey Canada is not affiliated with any particular religion or spiritual institution. We are a secular, non-profit organization that honours all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions. Our programs are open to people of all faiths and those who identify as secular or agnostic. Everyone is welcome here.

Program Questions

The best program for you depends on your interests and availability. The Inner Journey Intensive is a popular entry point — a 3.5-day residential retreat offering deep immersion. The 5 Elements Movement Meditation is wonderful if you prefer shorter workshops. Family Constellation Journey works beautifully for those specifically interested in healing family patterns. Browse all programs or reach out to us for personalized guidance.

Program costs vary depending on the length, type, and venue. The Inner Journey Intensive is $1,295 per person (with a $200 early bird discount when registering 6+ weeks in advance). As a non-profit, we keep costs as accessible as possible while ensuring quality facilitation and care. Financial assistance may be available for those with genuine need — please inquire when registering.

Program schedules vary throughout the year. The Intensive typically runs twice annually (spring and fall). Other programs are offered on a rotating basis. Check our Events Calendar for upcoming offerings, or contact us to be notified when specific programs are scheduled.

Absolutely! Many people bring friends and family members. However, we do ask that you maintain healthy boundaries during the program itself — we want everyone to have space to focus on their own journey without worrying about loved ones. This often deepens the experience and gives you more to share afterward. Please contact us to discuss group registrations.

Logistics

Registration is simple. Visit our Contact page and fill out the registration form, or call Robert Boggs at 647-850-9337. You can also email inner.journey.canada@gmail.com. Once we receive your registration, we'll confirm your spot and send you additional details.

For residential retreats, bring comfortable clothing for movement, any personal medications, toiletries, and a journal. Meals and dorm accommodation are included. Phones and laptops are requested to be used minimally to create a true retreat experience. Specific packing details are provided with your registration confirmation.

A non-refundable deposit of $250 is required to secure your spot. For further cancellation details, please contact us directly. In the event of illness or genuine hardship, we work case-by-case to find fair solutions.

We are committed to accessibility. Our facilities are accessible to and usable by people with disabilities. If you have specific accessibility needs, please let us know when you register so we can ensure appropriate accommodations.
