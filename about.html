About Inner Journey Canada

A non-profit, educational organization creating environments for learning, growth, and transformation

Our Story

Inner Journey Canada is the Canadian-based affiliate of the U.S.-based Inner Journey Institute, a non-profit, educational organization committed to creating environments in which people can learn and grow, emotionally and spiritually, and apply this development to daily living.

Founded by Michael Schiesser, who brings decades of experience in personal transformation with a Master of Arts in Psychology and a background as a Buddhist monk, police officer, and licensed therapist, facilitating transformational experiences since 1982.

What began as a movement in the United States grew organically into a thriving presence in Canada, serving the Ottawa/Gatineau community and beyond. We are not affiliated with any religious or spiritual institution, and we honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions.

Our Vision

A world where people are fully alive, self-aware and connected — and are acknowledged for who they are.

Our Values

Inclusiveness & Acceptance

We welcome people of all races, ethnicities and sexual orientation. Our facilities are accessible to people with disabilities.

Love & Compassion

At the heart of everything we do is a commitment to creating safe, loving spaces where transformation can unfold naturally.

Strength & Responsibility

We hold ourselves and our community accountable, fostering personal power alongside deep care for one another.

Clarity & Resolve

We are committed to honest, clear communication and steadfast dedication to our mission of service and growth.

The Inner Journey Institute

Inner Journey Canada is part of the broader Inner Journey Institute movement, with roots across North America. Our programs draw from decades of experience, wisdom, and proven methodologies developed within the Institute community. This connection allows us to offer experienced facilitation while remaining deeply rooted in the Ottawa/Gatineau region.
