Your Inner Journey Starts Here

Transformational retreats and workshops for people ready to grow, heal, and reconnect with themselves.

A non-profit community in the Ottawa/Gatineau area offering safe, supported environments for deep personal work since 1982.

Explore Programs Is This For Me?

Who Comes to Inner Journey?

People from all walks of life — at moments when something inside is asking for more.

If You're Feeling Stuck

Maybe you've been through a loss, a transition, or a quiet knowing that something needs to change. You don't need to have the answers — just the willingness to show up. Our programs meet you where you are, with experienced facilitators who've guided hundreds of people through these very moments.

If You're Deepening Your Practice

You've done inner work before — therapy, meditation, workshops — and you're looking for the next level. Our retreats offer a depth and experiential intensity that goes beyond talk. The 2:1 facilitator-to-participant ratio means this isn't a lecture. It's personal.

Our Programs

Different formats for different stages of your journey

Flagship Program 3.5-Day Retreat

Inner Journey Intensive

An immersive residential retreat that supports deep self-discovery, ignites spiritual growth, and opens hearts to love and joy. Combining depth, adventure, movement, and celebration to unleash your human potential.

Frequency: Twice yearly (spring & fall)

Workshop 3 Hours

5 Elements Movement Meditation

A soulful practice of movement, breath, and meditation bringing awareness to the five energies of the Taoist tradition — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water. Develop presence, vitality, and a deeper connection to your body and the natural world.

Frequency: Periodically throughout the year
Next Offering: Check calendar Learn More
Workshop 2-Day

Family Constellation Journey

Based on the work of German psychotherapist Bert Hellinger, this workshop examines life challenges through the lens of family and ancestral patterns. Combined with Inner Journey methods, it offers a powerful opportunity to find lasting solutions and end deep suffering.

Frequency: Once yearly (winter)

Facilitator Training
