The Inner Journey Intensive

Many of us focus on the external quest for success through money, jobs, and relationships, which often provide only fleeting satisfaction. A deeper self remains unfulfilled.

The Inner Journey Intensive is an experiential retreat that supports deep self-discovery, ignites spiritual growth, opens hearts to love and joy, helps transcend limitations, and provides tools for fuller living. It is an experience of personal discovery combining depth, adventure, movement, and celebration.

Through the Intensive, you will:

  • Deepen awareness of your inner self
  • Open your heart to compassion and love
  • Enrich your relationships with self and others
  • Appreciate the gifts of the moment
  • Create new possibilities for the future

We welcome people of all races, ethnicities and sexual orientation. We honour all spiritual and non-spiritual paths and traditions.
