Our Impact

Nearly three decades of transformational retreats and workshops

Real Voices, Real Transformation

For nearly three decades, Inner Journey has been creating safe, supportive spaces for personal transformation. Here's what our community has to say.

If you've attended an Inner Journey program and would like to share your experience, we'd love to hear from you. Your story could inspire someone on their own journey.

Share Your Experience

"The IJ intensive workshop was a pure blessing. I did several self-development workshops, and this one feels like the gathering of all the best activities or exercices from all the workshops I did, the cream of the cream. But, most of all, it wasn't the different activities that made the workshop exceptional (even though they were all great), but it was the people's presence (participants and staff) that was so totally exceptional. From the first minute to the last one, I felt totally accepted, welcomed, part of the community… but most of all, I felt totally immersed in deep and authentic love. Thank you from the bottom of my heart."

— Remi F.

"What transpired for me during the Inner Journey was a weekend of self-discovery that opened my eyes to the subtlety of the unconscious beliefs that shape how I view and act towards the world, my relationships and myself. The IJ facilitators provided me with pragmatic tools that helped me understand my decisions and live with more intention, rather than guided by beliefs that no longer served me. Throughout this journey, I connected with people who cultivate respect, compassion, knowledge, growth and openness, I rekindled my ability to be vulnerable, and I regained a greater sense of confidence, calm, and willingness to take risks. I discovered a more profound sense of agency and I consequently started making choices that opened up a deeper connection with others and myself, allowing me to live wholeheartedly."

— Philippe C.
